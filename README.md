# spark-panthera
a 30cm tall and 30 cm long robot quadruped powered by 60kg.cm torqued motors and later wheels and encoded motors. spark has 12000mAH of battery life to last it and its 12 motors and electronics 30-60 minutes
spark weighs arund 4 kliograms and should be around and likely vastly more capable than the dingo quadruped in its abilities and strength.

# the reason
this started out 2 years ago in 9th grade with a desire to make something real for myself.
the name, spark, is a direct reference to its value and creation to me.
it should cost around 400-500 dollars to build

# assembly
to assmbely spark, simply print out all the stl files and attach servos to the mount positions using screws and bolts (i use M3 bolts)
and manage your wires cleanly to not cause mobility problems

# wiring
as for the wiring
the servos and electronics will be powered by the same powersupply (parallel connected 7.4v 6000mah batteries)
only the yello/communication wire of the servos will go to the servo driver
while the rest of the servo power cables go to an independant power distrubution board for smooth and safe power transmission
the battery will also go through a step down regulator to feed the micro controller aswell as the servo driver, both indepedntaly
there will also be a lot of connectors used inplace of soldering for safety 
and please connect a switch capable of handling the voltage and amperage directly after the battery junction
